# 隨ｬ2遶 蝠・2

## (1)
$M_X(t) = E(e^{tX})$
$= \int_0^1 e^{tx} dx$
$= \left[\frac{1}{t}e^{tx}\right]_0^1$
$= \frac{1}{t}(e^t - 1)$

$$
\left. M_X'(t) \right|_{t=0} = \left. -\frac{1}{t^2}(e^t - 1) + \frac{1}{t}e^t \right|_{t=0}
= \frac{te^t - e^t + 1}{t^2}
$$
縺薙・縺ｨ縺榊・蟄・$te^t - e^t + 1 \xrightarrow{t \to 0} 0$
蛻・ｯ・$t^2 \xrightarrow{t \to 0} 0$ 繧医ｊ縲√Ο繝斐ち繝ｫ縺ｮ螳夂炊縺九ｉ

$$
\left. M_X'(t) \right|_{t=0} = \left. \frac{e^t + te^t - e^t}{2t} \right|_{t=0}
= \left. \frac{te^t}{2t} \right|_{t=0}
= \left. \frac{e^t}{2} \right|_{t=0}
= \frac{1}{2}
$$

繧医▲縺ｦ $E(X) = \frac{1}{2}$

$$
M_X''(t) = \frac{t^2 \cdot t e^t - 2t (t e^t - e^t + 1)}{t^4}
= \frac{t^3 e^t - 2t^2 e^t + 2t e^t - 2t}{t^4}
$$
縺薙・縺ｨ縺榊・蟄・ $t^3 e^t - 2t^2 e^t + 2t e^t - 2t \to 0$
蛻・ｯ・ $t^4 \to 0$ 繧医ｊ縲√Ο繝斐ち繝ｫ縺ｮ螳夂炊縺九ｉ

$$
M_X''(t) = \frac{3t^2 e^t + t^3 e^t - 4t e^t - 2t^2 e^t + 2 e^t + 2t e^t - 2}{4t^3}
= \frac{t^3 e^t + t^2 e^t - 2t e^t + 2 e^t - 2}{4t^3}
$$
蜷梧ｧ倥↓縺励※ 蛻・ｭ・ $t^3 e^t + t^2 e^t - 2t e^t + 2 e^t - 2 \to 0$
蛻・ｯ・ $4t^3 \to 0$ 繧医ｊ

$$
M_X''(t) = \frac{3t^2 e^t + t^3 e^t + 2t e^t + t^2 e^t - 2e^t - 2t e^t + 2e^t}{12t^2}
= \frac{t^3 e^t + 4t^2 e^t}{12t^2}
= \frac{t}{12} e^t + \frac{1}{3} e^t
$$

繧医▲縺ｦ $M_X''(0) = \frac{1}{3}$

繧医▲縺ｦ $V(X) = E(X^2) - E(X)^2$
$= M_X''(0) - M_X'(0)^2$
$= \frac{1}{3} - \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^2$
$= \frac{1}{12}$

## (2)
$Y = X^2$, $Y$ 縺ｮCDF繧呈ｱゅａ繧・$F_Y(y) = P(Y \le y)$
$= P(X^2 \le y)$
$= P(-\sqrt{y} \le X \le \sqrt{y})$縲・x>0$ 繧医ｊ
$= P(0 < X \le \sqrt{y})$
$= F_X(\sqrt{y})$

$$
F_X(x) = \int_0^x 1 dt, \quad 0 < x < 1
$$
$= [t]_0^x = x - 0$ 繧医ｊ
$F_Y(y) = \sqrt{y}$
$f_Y(y) = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}}, \quad 0 < y < 1$

$$
E(Y) = \int_{0}^{1} y \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}} \, dy
= \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{1} \sqrt{y} \, dy
= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{2}{3} y^{\frac{3}{2}} \right]_{0}^{1}
= \frac{1}{3} (1 - 0) = \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
E(Y^2) = \int_{0}^{1} y^2 \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}} \, dy
= \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{1} y^{\frac{3}{2}} \, dy
= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{2}{5} y^{\frac{5}{2}} \right]_{0}^{1}
= \frac{1}{5} (1 - 0) = \frac{1}{5}
$$

$V(Y) = \frac{1}{5} - \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2 = \frac{4}{45}$

## (3)
$Y = -\log(X)$ 縺ｮ縺ｨ縺阪・Y$ 縺ｮCDF繧呈ｱゅａ繧・$F_Y(y) = P(Y \leq y)$
$= P(-\log(X) \leq y)$
$= P(\log(X) \geq -y)$  $y = -\log(X)$ 繧医ｊ $y > 0$ 縺ｧ縺ゅｊ $-y < 0$ 縺ｨ縺ｪ繧・$= P(X \geq e^{-y})$
$= 1 - P(X < e^{-y})$
$= 1 - F_X(e^{-y})$
$= 1 - e^{-y}$

$f_Y(y) = \frac{d}{dy} (1 - e^{-y}) = e^{-y} \quad (0 < y < \infty)$

$$
E(Y) = \int_{0}^{\infty} y e^{-y} \, dy
= \left[-y e^{-y}\right]_{0}^{\infty} - \int_{0}^{\infty} -e^{-y} \, dy
= 0 + \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-y} \, dy
= \left[-e^{-y}\right]_{0}^{\infty}
= 0 + 1 = 1
$$

$$
E(Y^2) = \int_{0}^{\infty} y^2 e^{-y} \, dy
= \left[-y^2 e^{-y}\right]_{0}^{\infty} - \int_{0}^{\infty} -2y e^{-y} \, dy
= 0 + 2 \int_{0}^{\infty} y e^{-y} \, dy
= 2 \cdot E(Y) = 2
$$

繧医▲縺ｦ $V(Y) = 2 - 1^2 = 1$
