# 隨ｬ2遶 蝠・4

$X \sim f_X(x) = \frac{x+1}{2}, \quad -1 < x < 1$

$Y = X^2$ 縺ｫ縺､縺・※縺ｮ邏ｯ遨榊・蟶・未謨ｰ・・DF・峨ｒ豎ゅａ繧九・$$
\begin{align*}
F_Y(y) &= P(Y \le y) \\
&= P(X^2 \le y) \\
&= P(-\sqrt{y} \le X \le \sqrt{y}) \\
&= F_X(\sqrt{y}) - F_X(-\sqrt{y})
\end{align*}
$$
縺薙％縺ｧ縲・F_X(x)$ 縺ｯ
$$
\begin{align*}
F_X(x) &= \int_{-1}^x \frac{t+1}{2} dt, \quad -1 < x < 1 \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{t^2}{2} + t \right]_{-1}^x \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{x^2}{2} + x - \left( \frac{1}{2} - 1 \right) \right) \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{x^2}{2} + x + \frac{1}{2} \right) \\
&= \frac{x^2}{4} + \frac{x}{2} + \frac{1}{4}
\end{align*}
$$
縺ｧ縺ゅｋ縺九ｉ縲・$$
\begin{align*}
F_Y(y) &= \left( \frac{y}{4} + \frac{\sqrt{y}}{2} + \frac{1}{4} \right) - \left( \frac{y}{4} - \frac{\sqrt{y}}{2} + \frac{1}{4} \right) \\
&= \sqrt{y}, \quad 0 < y < 1
\end{align*}
$$
縺励◆縺後▲縺ｦ縲∫｢ｺ邇・ｯ・ｺｦ髢｢謨ｰ縺ｯ
$$
f_Y(y) = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}}, \quad 0 < y < 1
$$
譛溷ｾ・､縺ｨ蛻・淵繧呈ｱゅａ繧九・$$
\begin{align*}
E(Y) &= \int_0^1 y \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}} dy \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \int_0^1 y^{\frac{1}{2}} dy \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{2}{3} y^{\frac{3}{2}} \right]_0^1 \\
&= \frac{1}{3} (1 - 0) \\
&= \frac{1}{3}
\end{align*}
$$
$$
\begin{align*}
E(Y^2) &= \int_0^1 y^2 \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}} dy \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \int_0^1 y^{\frac{3}{2}} dy \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{2}{5} y^{\frac{5}{2}} \right]_0^1 \\
&= \frac{1}{5}
\end{align*}
$$
$$
V(Y) = \frac{1}{5} - \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2 = \frac{4}{45}
$$
