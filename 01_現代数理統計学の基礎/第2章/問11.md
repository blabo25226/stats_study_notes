## (1) $f(k)$ 縺檎｢ｺ邇・ｳｪ驥城未謨ｰ(PMF)縺ｧ縺ゅｋ縺薙→縺ｮ險ｼ譏・$f(k) = \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{k+1}, \quad k = 0, 1, \dots$

縺ｾ縺・$k_1 \le k_2$ 縺ｫ蟇ｾ縺励※

## (2) $G_X(s)$ 縺ｨ $M_X(t)$ 繧呈ｱゅａ繧・$X \sim f(k)$ 縺ｨ縺吶ｋ縲・遒ｺ邇・ｯ埼未謨ｰ $G_X(s)$ 縺ｯ縲・$G_X(s) = E(s^X) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{k+1} s^k$
$= \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{s}{2} \right)^k$
$= \frac{1}{2} \cdot 1 \cdot \frac{1 - 0}{1 - \frac{s}{2}} \quad \left( \text{縺薙％縺ｧ} \frac{s}{2} < 1 \therefore s < 2 \text{ 縺ｨ縺励◆} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{2}{2-s}$
$= \frac{1}{2-s} \quad (s < 2)$

遨咲紫豈埼未謨ｰ $M_X(t)$ 縺ｯ縲・$M_X(t) = E(e^{tx}) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} e^{tk} \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{k+1}$
$= \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{e^t}{2} \right)^k$
$= \frac{1}{2} \cdot 1 \cdot \frac{1 - 0}{1 - \frac{e^t}{2}} \quad \left( \text{縺薙％縺ｧ} \frac{e^t}{2} < 1 \therefore t < \log 2 \text{ 縺ｨ縺励◆} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{2}{2-e^t}$
$= \frac{1}{2-e^t} \quad (t < \log 2)$

## (3) 髫惹ｹ励Δ繝ｼ繝｡繝ｳ繝医ｒ豎ゅａ繧・荳闊ｬ縺ｫ $\frac{d^n}{ds^n} G_X(s) = E(X \cdot (X-1) \cdot (X-2) \cdots (X-(n-1)) s^{X-n})$ 縺ｨ縺ｪ繧九◆繧√・$E(X(X-1) \cdots (X-k+1)) = \left. \frac{d^k}{ds^k} G_X(s) \right|_{s=1}$ 縺ｨ縺ｪ繧九・
