## (2) $E(X^k)$ 繧呈ｱゅａ繧・$E(X^k) = \left. \frac{d^k}{dt^k} M_X(t) \right|_{t=0}$ 繧医ｊ

## (3) $Y = \sigma X + \mu$ 縺ｮ蛻・ｸ・螟画焚螟画鋤 $Y = \sigma X + \mu \quad (\sigma > 0)$ 繧定｡後≧縲・$x > 0$ 繧医ｊ $y > \mu$ 縺ｨ縺ｪ繧九・$x = \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma}$ 縺ｧ縺ゅｊ縲・\frac{dx}{dy} = \frac{1}{\sigma}$ 繧医ｊ

$f_Y(y) = f_X \left( \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right) \frac{dx}{dy}$
$f_Y(y) = \frac{1}{\sigma} \exp \left( - \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right)$

縺ｾ縺溘∫ｴｯ遨榊・蟶・未謨ｰ $F_Y(y)$ 縺ｯ
$F_Y(y) = \int_{\mu}^{y} \frac{1}{\sigma} \exp \left( - \frac{t-\mu}{\sigma} \right) dt$
$= \left[ -\exp \left( - \frac{t-\mu}{\sigma} \right) \right]_{\mu}^{y}$
$= -\exp \left( - \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right) - (-1)$
$= 1 - \exp \left( - \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right)$

繧医▲縺ｦ
$f_Y(y) = \frac{1}{\sigma} \exp \left( - \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right) \quad (y > \mu)$
$F_Y(y) = 1 - \exp \left( - \frac{y-\mu}{\sigma} \right) \quad (y > \mu)$
